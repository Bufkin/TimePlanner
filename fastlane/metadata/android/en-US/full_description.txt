Time planner is an application for convenient planning and tracking of your schedule.
The application contains templates that allow you to quickly make a schedule, you can also enable auto-repeat of the template and no longer worry about manually adding it.
Each task in the schedule has its own category and subcategory for structuring your actions.
According to the mimio of this, there is analytics in the application that will help you make your plans for the day more effectively

Time Planner is an application for convenient scheduling and tracking of daily tasks.

📱The application supports:
- Convenient division of tasks by priority, categories and subcategories 🔥
- Scheduled alerts about the start of events ⏰
- Built-in notes for tasks 📌
- Templates for everyday tasks 🏃
- Automatic repetition of template tasks 🔁
- Protection against erroneous task overlap 📐
- Maintaining analytics and statistics 📊
- Backup and transfer of data 💾
- More than 10 different color themes 🎨
- 6 languages 🌍
